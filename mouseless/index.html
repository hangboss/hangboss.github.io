Operation Guide

Key Description
`CapsLock` Recommended to use `CapsLock`, supports binding to Ctrl/Shift/Command/Option,
activate Mouseless by double clicking
`I/K/J/L` Move the mouse cursor
`1/2/3` Move the mouse pointer to the center of the specified screen, with a maximum support of three screens
`U/O` Scroll up/down
`Y/P` Scroll left/right
`N` Click the left mouse button
`M` Click the right mouse button
`B` Click and hold the left mouse button, release by clicking again
`F` Switch between fast and slow moving speed
`Esc/Space` Disable Mouseless

Screen Division

We divide the screen into 9 areas. After activation, pressing the corresponding keys (`Q/W/E/A/S/D/Z/X/C`) can quickly move the mouse to the specified area.

By pressing Q + E, quickly move to the highlighted area, allowing you to more quickly locate the element you need to access.
